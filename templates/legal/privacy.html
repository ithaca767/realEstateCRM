Privacy Policy

Ulysses CRM

Effective Date: January, 2026

Last Updated: March, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ulysses CRM, owned and operated by Ithaca Enterprises, LLC (“Ulysses,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users access and use the Ulysses CRM platform.

This policy applies solely to the Ulysses CRM software platform and does not apply to brokerage services, MLS systems, open house sign-ins, or third-party websites.

1. Information We Collect
Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, login credentials, role designation, and account settings.

CRM Data Entered by Users

Users may enter and store contact details, transaction records, property information, engagement history, tasks, notes, and related real estate business data (“Customer Data”). This data is created, controlled, and managed by the user.

Email Integration Data

If you connect a Gmail or Outlook account through OAuth authorization, we may retrieve and store permitted email headers, metadata, and message content solely to provide CRM functionality. OAuth tokens are securely stored and used only for authorized access.

AI Processing Data

Ulysses CRM integrates with OpenAI services to provide optional AI features, such as summaries and search assistance. When you initiate an AI feature, selected CRM content may be transmitted to OpenAI for processing. AI processing occurs only when triggered by the user. AI outputs may be stored within the system.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide CRM functionality
  • Enable email synchronization
  • Support AI features
  • Maintain system security and performance
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell user data and do not use CRM data for advertising purposes.

3. Multi-Tenant Design and Data Isolation

Ulysses CRM operates as a multi-tenant platform. Each account’s data is logically isolated from other users. Users cannot access the CRM data of other users.

Administrative Access Controls

Ithaca Enterprises, LLC does not routinely access, review, or monitor user-entered Customer Data.

Administrative access may occur only when reasonably necessary to:

  • Provide technical support at the user’s request
  • Investigate security incidents or suspected misuse
  • Maintain, repair, or improve system functionality
  • Comply with applicable law or lawful governmental requests

We do not share one user’s CRM data with another user.

4. Data Hosting and Infrastructure

Ulysses CRM is hosted on infrastructure provided by Render. Data is stored in managed PostgreSQL databases operated by Render.

We implement commercially reasonable safeguards, including encrypted HTTPS transmission, authentication controls, and logical tenant isolation. No system can guarantee absolute security.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

We utilize third-party providers including:

  • Render (hosting and database infrastructure)
  • OpenAI (AI processing services)
  • Google and Microsoft (OAuth authentication services)

These providers process data solely to provide requested services.

6. Data Retention

We retain data while your account remains active and as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations.

You may request account deletion by contacting us. Certain records may be retained for compliance, audit, or security purposes.

7. Data Backup and Recovery

Ulysses CRM relies on third-party infrastructure providers for hosting and database services. While commercially reasonable measures are taken to maintain system integrity and availability, Ulysses CRM does not guarantee the availability, recoverability, or restoration of Customer Data.

Users are responsible for maintaining independent records and backups of critical business data. Ulysses CRM is not responsible for data loss resulting from user actions, third-party service disruptions, system errors, or unforeseen events.

8. User Responsibility for Data

Users are responsible for the accuracy, legality, and regulatory compliance of Customer Data entered into the system. Ulysses CRM does not verify or supervise user-entered data.

9. Children’s Privacy

Ulysses CRM is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of updates.
