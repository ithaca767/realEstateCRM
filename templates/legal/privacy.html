Privacy Policy

Ulysses CRM

Effective Date: January, 2026

Last Updated: March, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Ulysses CRM, owned and operated by Ithaca Enterprises, LLC ("Ulysses," "we," "us," or "our"), collects, uses, stores, and protects information when users access and use the Ulysses CRM platform.

This policy applies only to the Ulysses CRM software platform. It does not apply to brokerage services, MLS systems, open house sign-ins, or third-party websites.

1. Information We Collect
Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, login credentials, and account settings.

CRM Data Entered by Users

Users may enter and store contact details, property information, transaction records, notes, engagement history, tasks, and related real estate data. This information is entered and controlled by the user.

Email Integration Data

If you connect a Gmail or Outlook account through OAuth authorization, we may retrieve and store email headers, metadata, and message body content to support CRM functionality. OAuth tokens are securely stored and used solely for authorized access.

AI Processing Data

Ulysses CRM integrates with OpenAI services to provide optional AI features, including engagement summaries and AI-assisted search. When you trigger an AI feature, selected CRM content may be transmitted to OpenAI for processing. AI outputs and related representations may be stored within the system. AI processing occurs only when initiated by the user.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to provide CRM functionality, enable email synchronization, support AI features, maintain system security, improve reliability, and comply with legal obligations.

We do not sell user data and do not use CRM data for advertising purposes.

3. Data Hosting and Infrastructure

Ulysses CRM is hosted on infrastructure provided by Render. Data is stored in managed PostgreSQL databases hosted by Render.

We implement commercially reasonable safeguards including encrypted HTTPS transmission, authentication controls, and logical tenant isolation. No system can guarantee absolute security.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

Ulysses CRM utilizes third-party providers including:

  • Render (hosting and database services)
  • OpenAI (AI processing services)
  • Google and Microsoft (OAuth authentication services)

These providers process data solely to provide requested services.

5. Data Retention

We retain data while your account remains active and as necessary to provide services or comply with legal obligations. You may request account deletion by contacting us. Certain records may be retained for compliance or audit purposes.

6. Multi-Tenant Design

Ulysses CRM operates as a multi-tenant platform. Each account's data is logically isolated and not accessible by other users.

7. Children's Privacy

Ulysses CRM is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of Ulysses CRM constitutes acceptance of updates.
